如图，易见\(\triangle AOM_1\)与\(\triangle BAM_1\)相似，
于是可以根据勾股定理和相似三角形建立关于\(x\)和\(y\)的方程\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{l}
		(r+x)^2+y^2=(2y)^2, \\
		\frac{y}{x}=\frac{r+x}{y}, \\
		x,y,r>0,
	\end{array} \right.
\]
化简得\[
	\left\{ \begin{array}{l}
		(r+x)^2=3y^2, \\
		y^2=x(r+x), \\
		x,y,r>0,
	\end{array} \right.
	\quad\text{或}\quad
	\left\{ \begin{array}{l}
		(r+x)^2=3x(r+x), \\
		x,y,r>0,
	\end{array} \right.
\]
于是有\(r+x=3x\)，
解得\(x=\frac12 r, y=\frac{\sqrt3}2 r\).
